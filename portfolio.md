# My Projects

Here is a high level summary of the data science projects I have been working on in my free time. Links to all the repositories, found on my personal [GitHub profile](https://github.com/rhiannaleslie), are provided for a more in-depth look at the methods used.


## Visualising my backpacking trip

From April to September 2025 I backpacked through 8 countries in Southeast Asia, keeping a journal of every place I stayed along with a personal rating. I used the Google Places API to enrich this data with coordinates and Google review scores, then built a pipeline to refresh the enrichment and render it as an interactive Folium map.

## Data Science for Policy: Energy Prices and CPI Trends in Great Britain

Completed during the ONS Data Science in the Public Sector graduate programme, this project applied k-means clustering to UK sector CPI datasets to explore whether similar inflation patterns could be identified across sectors during the 2022 energy crisis. Clusters were evaluated using silhouette scores and compiled into a presentation with recorded verbal commentary.
